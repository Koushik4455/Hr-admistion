GenesisHR Battlegreen Run

Since 1996, the annual GenesisHR Battlegreen Run has raised funds for affordable housing, college scholarships, cancer research, and many other causes. Each fall, more than 1,000 runners and walkers participate in the run, which is held in the heart of historic Lexington, Massachusetts.

In support of this effort, our employees helped form the Battlegreen Run Foundation, a 501c(3) nonprofit organization that provides a platform for other nonprofits who use our race to raise funds and awareness for their missions as well.

Proceeds from this event also support our legacy partners – Habitat for Humanity of Greater Boston, the Ovarian Cancer Education Awareness Network, the Pauline Briggs Memorial Scholarship Fund, the Genesis Community Fund, and other very worthy causes.

Serving our community

Each year, Genesis HR employees are offered one paid community service day to serve however they choose—in the past, our team members have delivered turkey meals on Thanksgiving day, assisted at local food banks, and delivered flowers for local nursing homes for the day of service.

In addition, our employees and our company give both time and financial support and participate in numerous charitable organizations and causes important to them. Our team members are proud to support causes like Habitat for Humanity, Burlington Public Schools donations/support, Boys & Girls Club of Stoneham, Fed Up With Cancer, and more.

“For us, identifying needs of our community and finding ways to make an impact is not only the right thing to do, it just makes good sense. Being an active and proud member of our community is an integral part of the fabric of GenesisHR and will always be a cornerstone of who we are as a company.”
Bob Burbidge, CEO
